## ⚙️ Core Technical Features (Across All Files)

- **60 FPS Physics Particles**: Uses a canvas-based simulation of rising golden embers/bokeh. Velocities are normalized using delta-time equations to ensure the movement speed is identical across standard 60Hz and high-refresh-rate 120Hz/144Hz monitors.
- **Input Locks & Cooldowns**: Navigation inputs are debounced and locked during active transitions (800–850ms) to ensure animations complete smoothly without double-clicks or skips.
- **Multi-Modal Controls**: Full support for Right/Left arrow keys, mouse wheel/trackpad scrolls, mobile touch swipes, and interactive progress bars at the bottom.
- **Preview Background Toggle**: A button in the top-right corner allows you to toggle between a royal blue radial gradient preview (matching the Netra theme) and a 100% `transparent` background for OBS overlays or system video embeds.
